Fruity Dessert is a type of meal taken after a main dish; it consists of fruits covered with various seasonings.They are rich in vitamins,which are vital in preventing certain diseases.

Four Types Of Fruity Desserts

  • Honey Rhubarb Brown Betty
  • Plum Berry Crisp
  • Cherry Coconut Crisp
  • Nectarine Brown Butter Buckle

Ingredients For Cherry Coconut Crisp

  • Cherry Filling;coconut flour,honey,fresh lemon juice, lemon zest.
  • Crisp Topping;almond flour, flaked coconut,sliced almonds,oats,honey ,cocnut oil melted or cooled
